Culture & Traditions

🎉 Lunar New Year

Families reunite, lanterns glow, and firecrackers light up the streets to welcome prosperity and good fortune.

🚣‍♂️ Dragon Boat Festival

High-speed boat races and sticky rice dumplings celebrate tradition, legend, and community spirit.

🎶 Indigenous Music & Art

Explore rich tribal heritage through expressive dances, music, and intricate handcrafted artwork.

🏯 Temple Architecture

Elaborate carvings, bright colors, and spiritual symbolism decorate Taiwan’s historical temples.
