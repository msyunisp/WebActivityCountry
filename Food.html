Taiwanese Food

Beef Noodle Soup

The soul of Taiwanese comfort food — rich broth, tender beef, and chewy noodles. A must-try for first-timers!

Bubble Tea

This globally famous drink was born in Taiwan! Sweet milk tea with chewy tapioca pearls. You can't leave without it.

Lu Rou Fan

Savory braised pork belly over rice — warm, filling, and flavorful. Taiwanese comfort food at its finest.

Stinky Tofu

Don’t let the smell scare you — this fermented favorite is crispy outside, soft inside, and totally unique. Dare to try?

Oyster Omelet

A crispy, savory delight made with fresh oysters, eggs, and a tangy sauce. A true night market classic you shouldn't miss!
